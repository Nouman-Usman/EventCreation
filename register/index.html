Register for CipherCraft

Before registering, make sure to read and comply to the following rules and guidelines regarding the event:

Registration
  1. By registering and making a payment you agree that all details provided are accurate. Any discrepancy may result in cancellation of registration.
  2. Cancellation and rescheduling of registration are not allowed.
  3. All tickets are non-refundable, non-transferable and is valid for one attendee only.
  4. Holder of any duplicate or forged passes/badges/tickets will be handed over to the security and/or police personnel. Forging passes is a criminal offence.
Venue
  1. We urge you to mark your presence at the venue 45 minutes prior to event timing to carry out necessary verification.
  2. All attendees must carry e-ticket or its printout for verification at the venue.
  3. Please carry a valid government identity card to attain a conference attendee badge at the venue.
  4. The attendees must wear their conference badge throughout the event.
  5. Seat numbers will be allotted to each confirmed and verified attendee at the entrance.
  6. Entry into the venue will only start 30 minutes before the event timing.
  7. The venue doors will remain shut during hackathon and will not open for entry.
  8. Attendees reaching late at the venue will not be allowed to attend the event.
  9. Re-entry into the venue is strictly prohibited.
Appeal
  1. The organisers reserve the rights of frisk and restrict entry.
  2. Please cooperate with the private security and police personel or any agencies for security checks.
  3. Any individual might be asked to leave the venue if there is any behavioural misdemeanour. This decision rests with the conference and venue management and is final and binding.
  4. Carrying of food items, alcohol, cigarettes, weapons, inflammable and banned substances are strictly prohibited.
  5. The organiser does not take any responsibility for injuries, loss or theft of any personal belongings of the ticket holder.
  6. There will be adequate breaks for networking and refreshments.
  7. No children under the age of 15 will be allowed at the venue unaccompanied.
  8. All standard venue and attendee rules are to be adhered to by attendees.
Socialise
  1. Use the official hashtag #CipherCraft on social media to talk about the event.
  2. Most importantly, have a great time!
