Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a 36-hour event where students come together to create a project from scratch. It is a great opportunity to learn new skills, meet new people, and have fun!

How much will it cost?

Cipher Craft is completely free for all participants. We will provide food, drinks, and swag for all participants. We do not provide travel stipends.
